Principles of Design

PARC: Proximity

GPS Farma

https://gpsfarma.com/

The principle of proximity is applied in the grouping of the products that are sold and how they are related to each other, colors and lines are used a lot to determine the relationship that exists between them.

PARC: Alignment

Al Mundo

https://almundo.com.ar/

In each tour package, the title, content and price are aligned to the right. This helps to follow a line and know that the content is accurate and clear.

PARC: Contrast

Pago Mis Cuentas

https://pagomiscuentas.com

On this page the contrast is given in the logo with the color blue and orange. As well as throughout the page these two colors predominate, offering a strong visual memory to be able to relate the brand with these colors.
