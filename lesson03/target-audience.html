Target Audiencie

The target audience It is a group of people who want to be part of a community that supports them to carry out activities to share with other people who have the same interests.

Two Personas

Person 1

Raul is a 56-year-old man who owns a small family business and would like information on the procedures he needs for his company to comply with the requirements to legally sell its products. He would also like to be informed about the training that he can obtain in order to specialize and be more competitive in the labor market. He likes to receive new ideas that help him innovate and help his company grow. He always has his cell phone at hand to be in contact with his employees, customers and suppliers, as well as receive news about the latest trends.

Person 2

Adriana is a 45-year-old married woman with two children who works in a public ministry. She is interested in the information that she can obtain both for her work and for recreation. She generally uses a desktop computer since her work requires it, and she can be informed of the news of the municipality

Scenarios

1- A producer who wants to distribute his products in the city.

2- An executive who wants to implement corporate social responsibility.

3- A processed food company that seeks to incorporate healthy foods into its production.

4- A cosmetics company that wants to improve the packaging of its products through recycling.

5- A content creator who wants to have contact with affiliated companies.
